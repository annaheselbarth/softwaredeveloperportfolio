Hi, I'm Anna Heselbarth

My journey into the tech world has been quite interesting. I have been running my own health coaching business for the past 3+ years. It has been amazing to help so many to have better quality of life and thrive again. As much as I love coaching, I was surprised to find that once I moved my business online that it would lead me down the road into the tech field. At first, tech seemed frustrating as I had never studied it before but then I took an introductory course into software development and immediately fell in love with programming.


And as I do with all things that I love... I dove right in and signed up for a software development bootcamp. I could see the endless possibilities of being able to make an even greater impact in the world through coding. I was even able to combine my two passions for my final project in software development bootcamp, which was a Food Trial Helper MVC application. This application will help those struggling with food allergies and intolerances to be able to keep track of their trials, their safe foods, and what medicines were helpful (if needed) so that they could better communicate with their health team.


I'm so thankful that software development crossed my path and am excited to work with others to make products that will impact the world. Thank you for taking the time to check out my portfolio.
